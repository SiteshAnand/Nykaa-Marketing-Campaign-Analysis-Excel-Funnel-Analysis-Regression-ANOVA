# 💄 Nykaa Marketing Campaign Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes a simulated e-commerce marketing dataset (Nykaa) to evaluate campaign performance, customer behavior, and revenue drivers using Excel-based statistical techniques.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze campaign performance across multiple channels
- Evaluate campaign funnel efficiency (Impressions → Clicks → Leads → Conversions)
- Identify key drivers of revenue
- Perform customer segmentation analysis
- Enable data-driven decision-making

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed
- **Funnel Analysis** (CTR, Lead Conversion, Drop-off Analysis)
- **Regression Analysis** (Leads vs Revenue)
- **ANOVA Testing** (Campaign & Customer Segments)
- **Correlation Analysis** (Engagement vs Revenue)
- **Revenue Forecasting**

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Leads are a **strong predictor of revenue (R² ~ 65%)**
- Each additional lead contributes **₹274.84 revenue**
- Conversion rate is **~55% (strong performance)**
- No significant difference across campaign channels
- Major drop-off observed at **Impressions → Click stage (CTR ~8.5%)**

---

## 📉 Business Recommendations
- Improve **ad creatives and targeting** to increase CTR
- Focus on scaling campaigns due to strong conversion engine
- Optimize funnel to reduce top-stage leakage
- Use leads as a key KPI for revenue growth
